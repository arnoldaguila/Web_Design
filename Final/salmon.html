Salmon

A Healthy and Filling 20-minute meal.

Ahh, Salmon. My favorite food. The only delicious “Healthy Food” I have ever eaten. Salmon can be cooked in many ways, but I found that Oven Cooked salmon taste better.

All You Need
Equipment Ingredients
Aluminum baking pan. Fillet of salmon.
Oven Salt
Paper Towels Pepper
Connola Oil

Attention:

When preparing the salmon make sure you don’t have any more pin bones in your fish. Also, I make salmon with the skin on it. Personal preference you can make salmon with skin off the same way I do.

If frozen thaw salmon. Let the salmon come to room temperature before cooking.

Oven Cooked

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Place salmon on the aluminum baking sheet.
  3. Pat salmon dry with paper towels.
  4. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
  5. Once the oven reaches 450 degrees Fahrenheit, place salmon in oven.
  6. Set an alarm for 20 minutes.
  7. Once the alarm goes off. Take the salmon out of the oven.
  8. Serve and Enjoy.
