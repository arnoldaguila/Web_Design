Steak

An Easy Big Meal.

Steak is a threating hunk of meat to a lot of people. I don’t know why it would be. It’s pretty easy to cook.

There is multiple ways to cook a steak. There is the gill method, thats pretty common and a pan fried steak, but I will give you steps to cook a steak in the Reverse Sear Method.

All You Need
Equipment Ingredients
Pan 1 inch Thick Steak
Tongs Conola Oil
Meat Thermometer Butter
Oven Salt
Aluminum Baking Pan with a Rack Pepper
Spoon
Attention: Make sure you have a meat thermometer that is oven safe and has an alarm that will go off when you reach the desired temperature.

Reverse Sear

Make sure your steak is thawed out if frozen. If not frozen, or just recently thawed, let your uncooked steak come to room temperature. That will usually take about 30 to 45 minutes.

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Place uncooked steak on the wire frame aluminum baking pan.
  3. Salt and pepper steak liberally on both sides.
  4. Place meat thermometer probe in steak before placing it in the oven.
  5. Set meat thermometer to 131 degrees Fahrenheit (Medium Steak).
  6. Once oven is at 275 degrees Fahrenheit place steak in the oven.
  7. Once the meat thermometer alarm is blaring take your steak out of the oven.
  8. Place pan on stove.
  9. Turn heat on highest setting.
  10. Pour in 2 table spoons of canola oil.
  11. Spread oil evenly at the bottom of the pan.
  12. Once oil looks shimmery place steak in the pan with your tongs and wait for 1 minute.
  13. Flip the steak, wait another 1 minute.
  14. Place 2 table spoons of butter on one end of the pan.
  15. Tilt to have all the liquid poor into a pool.
  16. With your spoon scoop and pour liquid over steak and repeat for 30 seconds.
  17. Flip steak.
  18. Repeat steps 15 and 16.
  19. Serve and Enjoy.
