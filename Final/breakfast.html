Breakfast

The Most Important Meal of The Day.

Breakfast. It kickstarts your body to wake up. Never Skip Breakfast. That is an unhealthy habit that can lead to high blood pressure, high cholesterol, hypertension, and even obesity.

Here is a simple meal to start your day.

All You Need
Equipment Ingredients
Pan Bacon
Spatula Eggs
Bowl Butter
Fork

Attention:

Before you start. Make sure you have all your equipment in front of you. This will eliminate the need to run around and grab things while cooking.

Scrambled Eggs

  1. Crack Eggs into bowl.
  2. Scramble Eggs with Fork until there is an even yellow color to the eggs.
  3. Place pan on stove.
  4. Stove heat on high.
  5. Place a tablespoon of butter on the pan.
  6. Spread butter till evenly coating the bottom of the pan.
  7. Pour scrambled eggs from bowl to the pan and season with salt
  8. Stir eggs with spatula.
  9. Keep stirring with a spatula till the eggs are cooked to the desired doneness.
  10. Serve and Enjoy.

Bacon

  1. Stove Heat Off.
  2. Place 4 strips of bacon on the pan.
  3. Turn heat On highest setting.
  4. Wait at least 5 minutes till you flip bacon with spatula.
  5. Wait 2 minutes after first initial flip.
  6. Let Cook to desired doneness.
  7. Serve and Enjoy.
